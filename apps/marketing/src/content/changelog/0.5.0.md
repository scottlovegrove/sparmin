---
version: 0.5.0
summary: The time is on screen, and a stray swipe can no longer end your session.
---

- **The clock is now on the main screen**, above the station strip — so you can
  check the time mid-session without leaving the app.
- **Swiping right no longer drops you out of a running session.** On touch
  watches that gesture is the system's "back", and on the home screen the watch
  answered it by closing the app — which cut the recording short and saved the
  activity as it stood. While a session is running the swipe is now ignored, with
  a reminder to use the **End** tile to finish. At the start screen it still
  leaves the app as before.
- **The end, discard and summary screens honour the same swipe.** Swiping right
  now means resume, cancel and dismiss respectively, instead of half-leaving a
  screen and stranding the session behind it.
